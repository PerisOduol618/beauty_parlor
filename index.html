ABOUT SALON SAFARI

The Salon Safari

Salon Safari is an Aveda Concept Salon located in Uptown Nairobi. All of our valued customers receive free parking.

Our carefully chosen and highly trained staff is here to exceed your expectations. The same goes for our elegant salon space – dedicated to guest comfort – and our devotion to innovation and artistry. We’re focused on making your day and continually raising the bar.

Mission

Our mission is to provide the highest quality service, in union with the highest quality products for the well being of our guests and the environment.

We share Safari’s exceptional standards of quality products, treatments, and guest care, and everything we do reflects the standards of an Aveda Salon.

Vision

To be the best salon globally

To have our customers satisfied and produce the best products that have a positive effect on their skin

Why we exist

Salon Safari is an inclusive beauty company focusing on the unique needs and desires of our guests, making us the most versatile salon: providing professional service, experience and results above and beyond expectations!

Escape from everyday busy reality and treat your skin with intensive care. Beauty and health go hand in hand. The combination of quality cosmetics and state-of-the-art aesthetic technologies creates an irresistible combination that leaves the skin soft, supple and nourished.
