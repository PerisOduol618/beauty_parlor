ABOUT SALON SAFARI

The Salon Safari

Salo Safari is an Aveda Concept Salon located in Uptown Nairobi. All of our valued customers receive free parking. Our mission is to provide the highest quality service, in union with the highest quality products for the well being of our guests and the environment.

Why we exist

Versa Salon is an inclusive beauty company focusing on the unique needs and desires of our guests, making us the most versatile salon: providing professional service, experience and results above and beyond expectations!
